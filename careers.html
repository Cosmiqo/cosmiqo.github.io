Cosmiqo is a humble start-up that creates innovative technology simply to make people smile more. We are always nice to sentient beings and place ethics at the core of our values. Engineers at heart, the team is always looking out for unique ways to enhance the quality of life.
We're hiring

There are some pretty fun and pioneering projects up in the pipeline. From sensor-fusion platforms to geospatial analytics, we are in a position to meddle around with and develop some mind-numbing applications.

If you resonated while reading this, you, like us, would never have to work a day in your life (Confucius was a smart man) and do consider joining us.
