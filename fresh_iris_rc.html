Universal and Undispusted Platform for your Robotics Fleet

IRIS Robot Controller enables faster, more accurate, and flexible robot operations in order to attain greater warehouse throughput.

Cross-platform coordination

One robot controller for any type of robot, brand or operations. Never be hardware and system-dependent again.

Supercharge warehouse velocity

Increase robot productivity with greater job coordination and optimised periphery actions for higher throughput and quicker robot ROI.

Global view of operations

Track fleet status in real-time and simulate operations in advance to determine optimal asset allocation.

Name Says It All

IRIS Robot Controller is the only software solution you will ever need to execute robot operations effectively.

Fleet Management

A single software platform that does job assignment, traffic control, collision avoidance and charging for ongoing fleet operations.

Artificial Intelligence

We unlock the full potential of your robotics fleet with IRIS A.I. by performing dynamic path generation & job reassignment, opportunistic charging and robot placement - all this while remaining deadlock-free.

Hassle-free Usage

IRIS Robot Controller can work in tandem with any warehouse management system, robot and robot management system, and can even replace legacy robot management systems as a robot management platform. 3 separate systems streamlined and managed by 1 seamless platform. Little or no user input is required for the IRIS Robot Controller to work.

Forward Planning

Minimize disruptions to operations when making asset adjustments by using IRIS Robot Controller's simulation functions to plan different home and charging locations, number of robots and charging stations, as well as different warehouse layout and demand patterns.

Designed to Fit Your Robot Fleet Operation Needs

IRIS Robot Controller provides the software platform for the optimal orchestration, optimization and visibility of assets required for robot warehousing operations.
