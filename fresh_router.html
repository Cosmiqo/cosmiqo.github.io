IRIS Router. Warehouse Pick Path Optimisation.

Be Efficient.

IRIS Router™

Order picking has been identified as a major activity within a warehouse and it consumes about 55% of total warehouse operating costs. Additionally, pickers spend about 55% to 70% of time travelling during order picking. IRIS Router increases picking efficiency by providing superior pick-paths that reduces the travelling distance.

Performance-based

Well-designed analytics engine engine

Purpose

IRIS Router saves you time by producing an efficient picking path for a very large number of orders in a warehouse.

Benefits
