Increase pick productivity by as much as 40%

Less walking results in less fatigue for employees, allowing for a happier workforce that are more likely to stick around.

Reduce the need for overtime hours significantly

Eliminate bias in the assignment of roles

Allocate workloads based on effort required and individual attributes rather than inherent and inefficient preferences.

Instant distance reduction from new pick lists created allow significant efficiency gains.

Increase warehouse throughput without additional resources

No additional hardware, manpower required to achieve greater warehouse velocity.

Save significantly on hardware cost and troubles down the road

Future-ready platform is good to go for years
