The Team

Founding members

Dr Koh Niak Wu
Founder + CEO

Niak Wu specialises in supply chain analytics, strategy execution and operations management innovation. He was part of the Global Supply Chain and Logistics team at Dell where he contributed to strategy analytics. Prior to that, Niak Wu was with the Planning and Operations Management Group in SIMTech, A*STAR where he gained valuable insights into the evolution of industries.

As an Adjunct Faculty at both the Singapore Management University and UniSIM, he shares his views and cross-industry experiences in supply chains and operations management. Niak Wu hopes that the next generation of industry leaders will be the change that we need.

He codes in Python, believes in the power of operational analytics and how it shapes business decisions.

Dr Melvin Zhang
Co-founder + CTO

Melvin is an avid programmer who enjoys designing and implementing novel algorithms. Fomerly from Hoiio, he was building APIs that allows developers to easily add telephony capability to their apps. As the lead architect of Cosmiqo, Melvin is in charge of developing its sensor data aggregation and analytics platform.

Melvin is actively involved with Friday Hacks, a weekly meeting organised by NUS Hackers (which is a student-run organization committed to the spread of hacker culture & free/open-source software) and Hackers & Painters (a lunch-time skills sharing event for UI/UX designers and software/hardware hackers to interact and share their skills and know-how in an informal setting).

In his spare time, he works on the AI for Magarena, an open source implementation of Magic: The Gathering focused on creating a strong AI player.

Advisory board

Foo Kim Peng

Mr Foo is the Managing Director of Kerydh Pte Ltd, which focuses on business consultancy and investments. Fomerly holding a dual portfolio, he was the Chief Executive Officer of TransitLink Pte Ltd (a transport acquirer in the public transport sector) and a Director in the Land Transport Authority of Singapore. Prior to that, Mr. Foo was the General Manager of Stelcomms Pte Ltd, a subsidiary of Singapore Technologies (Electronics), which develops specialised communications systems.

A former pilot with the Singapore Airforce and retiring as a Colonel, he is currently involved with budding startups as an investor, advisor and mentor. Mr Foo is a trained executive coach and has been coaching mid-level officers for senior appointments in the SAF. He has extensive experience in developing and managing high technology products and systems especially in defence, communications, e-payments and public transport industries.

Dr Marcelo H. Ang Jr

Dr Ang is an Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering at the National University of Singapore with a Joint Appointment at the Division of Engineering and Technology Management and the Acting Director of the Advanced Robotics Centre.

His research interests span the areas of robotics, mechatronics, and applications of intelligent systems methodologies. Dr Ang also teaches at both the graduate and undergraduate levels in the following areas: robotics; creativity and innovation, applied electronics and instrumentation; advanced computing; product design and realization. He is also active in consulting work in these areas. In addition to academic and research activities, Dr Ang is actively involved in the Singapore Robotic Games as its founding chairman and the World Robot Olympiad as a member of the Advisory Council.

His work experience includes heading the Technical Training Division of Intel's Assembly and Test Facility in the Philippines, research positions at the East West Center in Hawaii and at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and a faculty position as an Assistant Professor of Electrical Engineering at the University of Rochester, New York.
