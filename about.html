About Cosmiqo

A team of technology enthuasists that specialises in supply chain analytics and operations management innovation.

Founded in 2013 in the trade hub of Singapore, Cosmiqo seeks to empower global supply chains with analytics and optimization engines enabling increased productivity, orchestration and visibility for supply chain operations. With one warehouse, one robot fleet and one data point at a time, Cosmiqo is transforming supply chains with data and A.I. and the outlook for many large global businesses.

Our goal is to realise tomorrow’s fulfilment, today.

Logistics is one of the most integral part of the global economy.
While the rest of the world continues to progress at a rapid pace,
data-driven decision making has remained elusive for most large enterprise supply chains.
