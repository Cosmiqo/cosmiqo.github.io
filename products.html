Automated Light Measurement Suite (ALMS)

Purpose
ALMS is a lighting measurement ecosystem designed to provide analytical insights into the performance of street lamps across the nation. It provides system alerts down to the level of lamp pole IDs based on GPS coordinates.
Productivity Gain
97%
Usage
Nationwide in Singapore
