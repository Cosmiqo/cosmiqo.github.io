Street Lighting from Kota Damansara to Centrepoint Bandar Utama.

Why we do what we do.
5 March 2016

After producing a light map of the roads around Kota Damansara with the awesome folks from the Sinar Project, we decided to continue using Litesense® for the journey back to Centrepoint Bandar Utama, our earlier rendezvous location.

We asked Waze for the best way back and it responded with a perfect route.

In the map below, the

  • green marker is the start point
  • red marker is the end point
  • different colours represent varying brightness levels (measured in lux)

Indicators for brightness level are

  • 0 ≤ lux < 3 (darkest)
  • 3 ≤ lux < 6 (darker)
  • 6 ≤ lux < 10
  • 10 ≤ lux < 20
  • 20 ≤ lux < 40
  • 40 ≤ lux < 60 (brighter)
  • lux ≥ 60 (brightest)

Please feel free to interact with the map.
